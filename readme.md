# Modular Multiplication Around A Circle
I saw this on [a vsauce D.O.N.G](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eXUUClgd-Eo&t=68s) and decided to implement it in Go

## controls
keys|function
-|-
q/a | +/- 100 points
w/s | +/- 10  points
e/d | +/- 1   points
u/j | +/- 100 multiplier
i/k | +/- 10  multiplier
o/l | +/- 1   multiplier
